Stone Bridge High School Capture the Flag

For Members of the Programming and Robotics Club

About

  • SBCTF is a cybersecurity competition for students at Stone Bridge High School.
  • SBCTF will run from 7:00 PM April 8 - 7:00 PM April 14.

Rules

  • Teams will be limited to three or fewer students.
  • Asking for help, within reason, is permitted.
  • Flags can be tested on the challenge pages.
  • Flag sharing will not be permitted.
